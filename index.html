Information Architecture

M. Afzal Bhatti

Objectives

The aim of lecture topics of this course is to introduce how to do Analysis and Design of a sofwtare system using Structured approach. During the course students will learn differnt activities being performed and outcome of the activities being presented using differnt visual presenations. Lectures will illustrate how to create Data Flow Diagrams (DFD) and Structure charts through simple examples and case studies.
